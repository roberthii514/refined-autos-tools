SEO guide

What to check in Mangools and what to do before publishing.

Keyword qualification in Mangools
v
Search volume >= 100/month
Below 100 means almost nobody searches for it. Even ranking number 1 gets you 5-20 visits/month.
v
KD <= 39
KD 0-25 is ideal. KD 26-39 is acceptable. KD 40+ means huge sites dominate -- you cannot rank yet.
v
At least 2-3 results in top 10 with DA under 30
If the entire top 10 is Car and Driver (DA 80) and Edmunds (DA 75), skip it.
!
Google the keyword -- check intent
Are top results articles or videos? You want keywords where top results are blog articles.
!
Regional keywords are easier to rank
Best used BMW California competes only locally -- lower KD, same buyer intent.
Before publishing -- WordPress checklist
v
Install Rank Math (free)
Handles meta descriptions, XML sitemap, on-page SEO scoring. Aim for score 70+ before publishing.
v
Set font size 18-20px, line height 1.6
Direct Mediavine recommendation. Add to theme CSS: body { font-size: 18px; line-height: 1.6; }
v
Submit sitemap to Google Search Console
Do this the day you publish your first article.
v
Add 3-5 internal links to other articles
Link every new article to 3-5 older ones. Update older articles to link back.
