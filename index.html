SEO guide

What to check in Mangools and what to do before publishing every article.

Keyword qualification in Mangools
✓
Search volume ≥ 100/month
Below 100 means almost nobody searches for it. Even ranking #1 gets you 5–20 visits/month. Not worth writing. Find a broader version.
✓
KD ≤ 39
KD 0–25 is ideal. KD 26–39 is acceptable. KD 40+ means huge sites dominate — you cannot outrank them yet. Add a modifier: "used", "under $X", a specific year, or a location to find a lower-KD variation.
✓
At least 2–3 results in top 10 with DA under 30
If the entire top 10 is Car and Driver (DA 80), Edmunds (DA 75), Motor Trend (DA 72) — skip it. But if you see a small blog at DA 18 on page 1, Google is willing to rank smaller sites here.
!
Google the keyword yourself — check the intent
Are top results articles or videos? If all top 10 are YouTube videos, Google prefers video for that keyword. You want keywords where top results are blog articles and guides.
!
Regional keywords are easier to rank
"Best used BMW" competes globally. "Best used BMW California" competes only locally — much lower KD, same buyer intent, easier to rank. Use the region selector to append location to your keywords and article content automatically.
Before publishing — WordPress checklist
✓
Install Rank Math (free)
Handles meta descriptions, XML sitemap, on-page SEO scoring. Aim for Rank Math score 70+ before publishing.
✓
Set font size 18–20px, line height 1.6
Direct Mediavine recommendation. Larger text = more vertical height = more ad placements = higher RPM. Add to your theme CSS: body { font-size: 18px; line-height: 1.6; }
✓
Replace all [IMAGE:] placeholders with real photos
Use Unsplash.com or Pexels.com (free, licensed). Set alt text = primary keyword + description.
✓
Add 3–5 internal links to other articles
Link every new article to 3–5 older ones. Update older articles to link back. This is free and one of the most effective SEO tactics available to a new site.
✓
Submit sitemap to Google Search Console
Do this the day you publish your first article. Go to search.google.com/search-console and submit your sitemap.xml URL.
