$1,000/month roadmap

Your realistic path from 0 articles to $1k/month in ad revenue.

Target network: Journey by Mediavine (1,000 sessions / 30 days). Once approved, expect $15–25 RPM. To hit $1k/month consistently you need roughly 50,000–70,000 monthly pageviews.
Phase 1 — Months 1–2: Build the foundation
• Publish 3–4 articles per week using this tool
• Focus on "true cost of ownership" and "used car guides" — highest commercial intent
• Target long-tail keywords: "how much does a used Audi A4 cost to maintain"
• Aim for 25–30 published articles before month 2 ends
• Apply for Google AdSense at 20+ articles for early revenue while you grow
Phase 2 — Months 3–4: Chase 1,000 sessions
• Scale to 5–6 articles per week
• Add internal links between every article
• Submit sitemap to Google Search Console (free) to speed up indexing
• Write 3–5 "pillar" articles (3,000+ words) on broad topics
• Apply for Journey by Mediavine once you hit 800+ sessions
Phase 3 — Months 5–8: Scale to $1k/month
• Upgrade to Mediavine full (50k sessions) or Raptive (100k pageviews)
• Identify your top 10 performing articles and expand/update them
• Add comparison tables and pros/cons — increases time on page = higher RPM
• At $20 RPM × 50,000 sessions: $1,000/month achieved
